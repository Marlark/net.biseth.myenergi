Connect your Zappi EV charger and Eddi power diverter to Homey to get Insights, and control them the way you want.

Start and stop charging your car manually, on a schedule, or by other conditions in a Flow e.g. based on changing energy prices harvested from Tibber or your local power company.

Control your water heater or other heating sources with Eddi power diverter, and set the amount of solar energy you want to use to heat your water or charge your car.

Monitor power and energy consumption directly on each device or via an external Harvi energy sensor.
